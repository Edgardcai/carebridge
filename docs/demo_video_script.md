# 1-2 Minute Demo Video Script

## 0:00-0:10 Opening

Show splash and legal disclaimer.

Voiceover: "CareBridge helps recently discharged patients and family members
turn recovery instructions into daily actions. It is a reminder and logging
tool, not a medical advice app."

## 0:10-0:25 Patient setup and dashboard

Show sign in, patient list, and dashboard.

Voiceover: "After login, the patient profile shows recovery day, today's
pending tasks, completed tasks, the next appointment, and quick actions."

## 0:25-0:45 Task flow

Open Tasks, task detail, mark complete, add task.

Voiceover: "Tasks can be medication, visits, rehab, or notes. Each task stores
schedule, repeat rule, assignee, source document, and completion state."

## 0:45-1:05 OCR review

Open Scan Doc, show candidates, edit/uncheck, create tasks.

Voiceover: "OCR never saves automatically. The user reviews extracted items and
chooses which ones become recovery tasks."

## 1:05-1:25 Symptoms and timeline

Open Log, move pain slider, save, open timeline.

Voiceover: "The daily symptom log records pain, temperature, notes, and photos.
The timeline summarizes the past seven days for follow-up visits."

## 1:25-1:45 Family hub and close

Open Family, show team progress, latest log, and overdue tasks.

Voiceover: "The family hub gives carers a read-only overview of task progress,
responsibilities, overdue items, and the latest symptom update."

## 1:45-2:00 Architecture note

Show README or simple architecture slide.

Voiceover: "The frontend is connected through a repository and service-port
architecture, so Firebase, ML Kit OCR, notifications, and charts can be plugged
in cleanly by the other roles."
